# 🔹 NEO8 BUILDER PROMPT

## WORKFLOW #3A — BASIC AI RECEPTIONIST FLOW (ECONOMIC)

### Flow Name

**AI Receptionist – Basic**

---

## Purpose (Read This First)

This workflow is a **basic, cost-efficient AI phone receptionist**.

Its ONLY job is to:

* Answer inbound phone calls
* Collect lead information
* Provide very high-level service info
* Log everything
* Hand off to humans

This flow **does NOT sell, qualify, score, or decide anything**.
All decisions are made by humans after the call.

---

## How This Flow Is Triggered

* Incoming phone call to the **main business phone number**
* Neo8 receives the call webhook
* Neo8 checks the CRM setting:

  * If `ai_receptionist_mode = basic`
  * Route the call to **THIS workflow**

(There is a separate workflow for Premium — do NOT mix them.)

---

## Voice / AI Requirements

* Use **economic voice + speech-to-text** (Whisper or equivalent)
* Keep responses **short, scripted, and controlled**
* No long conversations
* No improvising

---

## AI BEHAVIOR RULES (VERY IMPORTANT)

The AI receptionist MUST:

### ✅ Always

* Greet the caller politely
* Speak clearly and professionally
* Ask one question at a time
* Keep answers short
* Stay neutral and helpful

### ❌ Never

* Never sell
* Never promise pricing
* Never book confirmed appointments
* Never make decisions
* Never qualify or disqualify leads
* Never update CRM status beyond logging

---

## Call Flow Logic (High-Level)

### 1️⃣ Greeting

Example:

> “Hi, thank you for calling [Company Name]. I’m the AI receptionist. I can help take your information and pass it to our team.”

---

### 2️⃣ Collect Lead Information

Ask for and capture:

* Full name
* Phone number
* Email address (if available)
* Brief reason for calling

If the caller refuses → still proceed politely.

---

### 3️⃣ Provide Basic Information (If Asked)

Allowed responses:

* General description of services
* Broad price ranges ONLY (no guarantees)
* Business hours
* Next steps (“A team member will follow up”)

If question is too complex:

> “I’ll make sure our team reviews this and follows up with you.”

---

### 4️⃣ Tentative Scheduling (Optional, Never Confirmed)

If the caller asks to schedule:

* Ask for preferred day/time
* Clearly say:

> “This is a tentative request and requires human confirmation.”

Mark all scheduling as **Pending Human Confirmation**.

---

### 5️⃣ End Call

Example:

> “Thanks for calling. Our team will review this and reach out shortly. Have a great day.”

---

## What Happens After the Call

The workflow must:

* Send full call data + transcript to the CRM
* Log the interaction as:

  * `ai_receptionist_call`
* Store:

  * Caller info
  * Summary
  * Any tentative scheduling notes

No follow-ups are sent automatically from this flow.

---

## Integrations (Mention Only, No Setup Here)

* Phone / Voice provider (e.g. Twilio)
* CRM API (log only)
* AI model (basic / economic tier)

All credentials live **inside Neo8**, not the CRM.

---

## What This Flow Does NOT Do

* No lead scoring
* No tagging logic
* No follow-up automation
* No email or SMS sending
* No pipeline movement
* No decisions

This flow **only captures and logs information**.

---

## Summary for Builder (Read Carefully)

* One phone number
* CRM decides Basic vs Premium
* This is the **Basic** version
* Simple script-style AI
* Capture info → log → hand off to humans
* Keep it clean and lightweight
