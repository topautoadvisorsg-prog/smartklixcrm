SETTINGS TAB — FINAL DECISIONS (AUTHORITATIVE)

We are still talking only about the Settings tab.

The goal is:

Remove duplicate logic

Remove UI that belongs elsewhere

Keep Settings clean, non-technical, and non-overlapping

Treat Settings as workspace configuration, not automation or execution

❌ REMOVE THESE ENTIRELY (NO REPLACEMENT)
1️⃣ Communication Templates (SMS / Email)

Examples currently shown:

SMS Appointment Reminder

SMS Invoice Due

Email Templates

Decision: REMOVE

Reason:

Templates already live in dedicated Email / SMS / Automation areas

Duplicates existing functionality

Creates confusion and split ownership

Settings ≠ messaging engine.

2️⃣ Billing & Tax Settings

Decision: REMOVE

Reason:

Billing, taxes, invoices, and payments are handled by Stripe

No calculation, enforcement, or execution happens in Settings

Any Stripe-related logic belongs in:

Payments

Invoices

Stripe-specific flows

Settings should not pretend to manage billing logic.

If Stripe needs configuration:

That lives either:

Inside Stripe itself

Or inside a future Stripe Connection UI, not Settings
