Recommendation:
Remove Redundant Approval: For outbound delivery flows (SMS, Email, WhatsApp) where the CRM has already performed all necessary approvals, you should:
Remove or bypass the entire review/approval node chain for these flows.
Retain review/escalation only for:
Payment event exceptions (e.g., failed/refunded payments)
AI receptionist/inbound automation (where human review may still be needed)
Minimal, Clean Architecture: Outbound flows should be:
Triggered by CRM (with all approvals complete)
n8n delivers the message using the CRM-provided content
n8n logs the delivery result back to the CRM
No message generation or approval logic in n8n for these flows
