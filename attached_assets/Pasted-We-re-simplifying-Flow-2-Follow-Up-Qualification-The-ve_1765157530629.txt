Here’s what we actually need:
1. Purpose

This flow is not trying to “intelligently qualify” leads on its own.

Its job is simple:

Identify which leads need follow-up.

Notify or assist the human rep.

Only send automated messages if a human explicitly triggers it.

Keep things clean and lightweight.

No AI qualification.
No lead scoring tiers.
No automated decision-making.

2. What counts as a lead needing follow-up

A follow-up candidate is:

Contact with tag "new-lead"

OR a lead that hasn’t been touched in X hours/days

OR a lead manually marked for follow-up

That’s it.

3. Flow Logic (Simple Version)
Trigger

Scheduled every 2 hours (or business hours only, we’ll finalize later).

Steps

Fetch contacts from CRM

Only leads (not customers, not won deals)

Identify who needs follow-up based on:

Lead is new

Lead hasn’t been updated in X time

Lead is tagged for follow-up

Lead is waiting on rep action

For each qualifying lead:

Add tag: "follow-up-needed"

Write activity log (follow_up_detected)

Notify rep via assist queue (simple, not AI qualification)

No scoring, no tiers, no classification.
4. Human chooses next action

The system will not decide if a lead is hot, warm, cold, stale, etc.

The human will decide:

Send first email

Send SMS

Call

Mark as contacted

Mark as qualified

Mark as not interested

Advance stage

5. Integration with Flow #1

Flow #1 tags "new-lead"
Flow #2 picks that up and updates "follow-up-needed"

Nothing else.

6. What to remove

Lead scoring (0–100)

Tier labels (Hot, Warm, Cooling, Stale)

“Drip campaign” logic

“Nurture bucket” logic

AI-generated classification suggestions

Any complex/automated pipeline flow

None of these belong in our system.

7. What to keep

CRM API endpoints
