Neo8/CRM Automation – Final Flow Checklist
1. Lead Intake Flow

Purpose: Capture all incoming leads, validate, and store in CRM.
Triggers:

Chat widget (website bot) submission

Contact Us form submission

Extended intake form submission

Phone call (AI Receptionist)

Email submission

Key Actions:

Validate required fields (name, email, phone, etc.)

Create or update contact in CRM

Assign tags (source, type) and owner

Log all interactions in CRM (audit-ready)

Deduplication optional (human review only, no AI auto-merging needed)

Tentative meeting creation: mark as “Pending Human Confirmation”

AI Receptionist provides general service info + informational price ranges, does not confirm meetings, does not handle payments

Routing to human agent after intake

2. Follow-Up & Qualification Flow

Purpose: Humans handle all follow-up, AI only provides context and suggestions.
Triggers:

Lead record created or updated in CRM

AI receptionist collected tentative meeting info

Lead flagged as warm/high-interest

Key Actions:

Assign follow-up tasks to humans (call, email, schedule meeting)

Update lead stage: cold → warm → qualified

Tag leads by interest, industry, urgency

Tentative meetings reviewed and confirmed by human

No automated scoring, no automated escalation for urgency

Logs all actions in CRM

Calendar Integration:

Tentative meetings sync to Google Calendar (reminders handled by Google)

3. AI Receptionist Flow

Purpose: Phone intake and lead capture via voice AI.
Trigger: Incoming call
Key Actions:

Greeting: “Hi, this is [Company Name] AI receptionist…”

Collect name, email, phone, brief inquiry

Provide general service info + price ranges only

Offer to schedule tentative meetings, log as “Pending Human Confirmation”

Route all call data to CRM and human agent

Hang up politely on irrelevant/unqualified calls

Voice option:

Standard: Whisper (economic)

Premium: ChatGPT All-in-One (paid)

No payments, no human takeover of call

4. Stripe Payment Flow

Purpose: Handle subscription and retainer payments for paying customers.
Triggers:

Opportunity/lead moves to “Closed-Won”

Optional triggers: subscription request, payment method update, invoice generated

Key Actions:

Map/create Stripe customer from CRM data

Create Stripe subscription (default_incomplete for SCA/3D Secure)

Listen for Stripe webhooks: invoice.payment_succeeded, invoice.payment_failed, customer.subscription.updated

Soft declines: retry automatically

Hard declines: human follow-up task

Failed payments: optional service suspension via Neo8 API

Logs all actions, events, outcomes in CRM (no sensitive info stored)

5. Audit & Login Flow

Purpose: Secure, auditable login and authentication flow.
Triggers:

User login attempt (username/password, SSO, API token)

Key Actions:

Credential validation + optional MFA

Session creation, RBAC enforcement

Log every login attempt: user, timestamp, method, outcome

Alert on suspicious activity (failed logins, unusual location)

Optional integration: Google, Microsoft SSO

Store logs in immutable, append-only format

Maintain GDPR/CCPA compliance

6. Google Workspace Integration Flow

Purpose: Automate and sync Google Workspace (Gmail, Calendar, Sheets, Docs, Forms) with CRM.
Triggers:

New or updated calendar events

Incoming emails

Google Sheets/Docs changes

Google Forms submissions

Key Actions:

Parse Gmail, create/update leads or contacts in CRM

Sync meetings with Google Calendar (updates + reminders)

Map Sheets updates to CRM objects (log audit trail)

Capture Forms submissions and update CRM

Handle errors, rate limits, and retries

Log all Workspace actions for audit

7. Master Architect Review Flow

Purpose: Human oversight for all critical automated actions.
Triggers:

Lead Intake actions requiring approval

AI Receptionist tentative meetings

Google Workspace automated updates

Follow-Up & Qualification automation

Key Actions:

Present pending actions on dashboard for approval/rejection/modification

Route approved actions to standard workflow

Route rejected actions to human for intervention

Log all decisions in immutable audit trail

Configurable thresholds: define which actions require review

Dashboard supports filtering, real-time refresh, and clear indicators

8. Memory / Knowledge Storage

Purpose: Long-term memory for AI and flows.
Key Actions:

Store AI-collected info, lead data, meeting details, and interactions

Provide historical context for future interactions

Integrated into CRM/Neo8 for all flows requiring context

Implementation Approach

Start with Lead Intake → test full capture and logging.

Move to AI Receptionist → ensure phone intake and tentative meeting flow works.

Follow-Up & Qualification → humans take over; validate Google Calendar sync.

Stripe Payment Flow → handled separately by human, CRM receives status updates.

Audit & Login → ensure compliance and monitoring.

Google Workspace Integration → test email, calendar, sheets, forms sync.

Master Architect Review → integrate review dashboard with all automated flows.

Memory → enable historical context for AI interactions.
