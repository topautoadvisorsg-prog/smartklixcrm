4️⃣ n8n — What Workflow Is Actually Needed

Here’s the important part you asked for.

❗ Key Insight

Zappier does NOT need to directly trigger n8n in most cases.

You only need n8n workflows for execution, not for information gathering.

✅ Recommended n8n Setup (Phase 1 – Correct & Minimal)
Workflow Type: “Approved Action Executor”

Trigger

Webhook from CRM

Fired only after queue approval

Payload Includes

action_type (send_email, update_status, create_invoice, etc.)

source = Zappier | Intake | Human

validated parameters

trace_id / ledger_id

Nodes

Webhook Trigger

Switch / Router

Route by action_type

Service Nodes

Email

WhatsApp

Payments

Calendar

Result Callback

POST back to CRM

Include success / failure / metadata

That’s it.

❌ What We Do NOT Build (On Purpose)

❌ Zappier → n8n direct automation

❌ Zappier executing workflows autonomously

❌ n8n polling Zappier

❌ n8n deciding logic

n8n is muscle, not brain.

5️⃣ Why This Is the Right Call (Reality Check)

From real-world patterns (teams using Zapier/Zappier + n8n):

Zapier-style tools are great at connectivity

They are terrible at governance

Teams that let Zapier execute directly lose control fast

Teams that gate execution through an internal queue scale safely

You’re building the second kind of system.
