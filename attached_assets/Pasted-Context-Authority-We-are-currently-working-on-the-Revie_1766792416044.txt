1️⃣ Purpose of the Review Queue (Non-Negotiable)

The Review Queue is the Automated Governance Processing Layer.

It exists only to surface AI-generated actions that require automated governance validation by the Master Architect AI.

Humans do NOT approve here.
Humans do NOT click actions here.
Humans observe only.

If a human needs to approve something, that happens before this layer (human-created actions) or after this layer (Ready Execution), depending on origin.

2️⃣ What Appears in the Review Queue
✅ Appears Here

Only AI-generated proposals

From AI Action CRM

From Automation / n8n

From System-initiated AI workflows

❌ Does NOT Appear Here

Human-created actions

Operator-initiated estimates

Operator-initiated payments

Operator-initiated edits

Any action already reviewed by a human

If a human created it, it does not need AI governance review.

5️⃣ Ledger Rules (Critical Clarification)

Everything is logged to the Automation Ledger, but ownership is strict.

Ledger Anchor (ONLY ONE)

If a Company exists → ledger is anchored to the Company

If no Company exists → ledger is anchored to the Contact

There are NO sub-entities

There is NO hierarchy

There is NO re-anchoring

Ledger Entries Created Here

AI proposal received

AI governance decision

Approval / rejection outcome

The Review Queue reads from and writes to the ledger.
It does not mutate business data.
