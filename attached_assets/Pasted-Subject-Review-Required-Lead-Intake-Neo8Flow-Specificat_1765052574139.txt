Subject: Review Required — Lead Intake → Neo8Flow Specification

Team,

Below is the specification for the Lead Intake event Smart Klix CRM will send to Neo8Flow.
Before we send this to the n8n builder, we need your review and confirmation.

Purpose

Smart Klix CRM will send a Lead Intake event to Neo8Flow.
Neo8Flow will simply receive it and trigger automation.
This is a one-way flow only.

Key Rules

No deduplication in intake — CRM will handle duplicate detection internally.

No merge logic in intake.

No decision-making in intake.

No callback from Neo8Flow to CRM.

CRM handles the authentication token. Neo8Flow does NOT validate it.

Neo8Flow only:

receives the payload,

validates required fields,

logs it,

triggers automation.

Required Steps on Neo8Flow Side

(For your awareness / validation)

Receive POST webhook from Smart Klix CRM

Validate required fields:

schema_version

event_type

idempotency_key

tenant_id

timestamp

source.channel

Require at least one identifier:

data.contact.email

OR data.contact.phone

OR data.contact.id

OR data.conversation.recording_url

Log/store raw payload (optional)

Route by event_type (only lead.intake right now)

Trigger follow-up automation (placeholder)

Respond 200 OK
