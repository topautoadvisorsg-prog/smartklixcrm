Purpose: Validate end-to-end execution of Neo8 as an orchestration layer using REAL payloads, REAL credentials, and REAL side effects (messages, logs, CRM updates).
Scope: Economic mode only. Premium explicitly excluded.

🧪 TESTING PHASE OBJECTIVE

Confirm that Neo8 executes correctly when triggered by CRM-like payloads, without AI content generation, without approvals, and with correct logging back to CRM.

This is execution testing, not architecture review.
