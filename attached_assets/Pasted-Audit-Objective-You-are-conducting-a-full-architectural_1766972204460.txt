Audit Objective

You are conducting a full architectural, behavioral, and governance audit of our AI Action Console as it exists today.
This is not a theoretical design exercise. You must document actual current behavior, actual permissions, actual queues, actual ledgers, and actual failure modes.

Assume this system is operating in, or is intended to operate in, a Level 3 → Level 4 Agency environment (Assisted → Autonomous Action).
Your job is to identify what we have, how it works, where it is unsafe, and what is missing to meet Neo8 standards.
